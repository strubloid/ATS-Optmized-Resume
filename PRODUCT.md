# Product

## Register

product

## Users

CurriculumOptimizer is for job seekers, engineers, and career-switchers who need to keep one truthful master resume while generating tailored CVs for specific job applications. They use the app in a focused workflow: add or edit a master resume, paste a job description, review suggested changes, and export a clean document.

## Product Purpose

The product helps users produce truthful, Applicant Tracking System-friendly CVs without losing control of their source resume. Success means the user can understand why a CV changed, see an estimated compatibility score, identify missing requirements, and export a clean PDF, DOCX, or Markdown file without hidden comments or fabricated experience.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, trustworthy. The product should feel like a careful document editor and resume review assistant, not a hype-driven AI generator.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a fake resume score gimmick, a dark-pattern upsell flow, a noisy SaaS dashboard, a one-click fabrication tool, or a generic AI chat wrapper. Avoid fake Pro locks, guaranteed score claims, over-decorated controls, and designs that obscure the source evidence behind suggestions.

## Design Principles

- Truth before optimization: show missing evidence instead of inventing skills.
- Explain the score: every score and suggestion needs a reason.
- Preserve the source: generated CVs never overwrite `resume.md`.
- Review like an editor: comments align to document sections and bullets.
- Export with confidence: clean exports contain only final CV content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. The app must support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, readable contrast, reduced motion preferences, clear labels, and text that remains usable on mobile screens.
